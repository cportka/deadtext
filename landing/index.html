    A bit like TextEdit or Notepad, but even simpler and with fewer features. No hidden text. No formatting. No settings to fiddle with. Just a textarea and your file. UTF-8 in, UTF-8 out.

    Same code, every platform: macOS, Windows, Linux desktop apps; a PWA you can install from any browser; iOS and Android apps.
